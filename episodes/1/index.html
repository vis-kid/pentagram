Questions:

  • It seems to me that you are doing design to make other people happy. Is that somewhat accurate?

  • In your Google Talk, you mentioned that you tricked the big guys—in so many ways. I was curious to hear more about that…

  • What were some key moments in your career? Some forks in the road that affected your professional trajectory the most?

  • In an era of super rapid change these days, how important is it to follow trends in your experience? Do you think we should more often aim to create something that can last longer?

  • Nowadays there are so many parts to a brand. Services and a whole ecosystem around a brand have gained a lot of weight. Do you think logos matter as much as they did in the past?

  • There seems to be a trend to redesign corporate identities every few years. What do you think about that approach?

  • What else are you passionate about that your audience usually doesn’t get to see?

  • Do you remember any specific situations that made you rethink your whole approach to work and maybe even life?

  • How do you keep a fresh perspective?
